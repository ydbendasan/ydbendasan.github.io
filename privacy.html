PegJump Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 14, 2026

PegJump does not collect, transmit, sell or share personal data with the developer. The game does not require an account and contains no advertising, analytics or tracking SDKs.

Data stored on your device

The game stores settings, course scores and compact replay inputs locally on your device. This information is used only to preserve your progress and can be removed by deleting the app or clearing its storage.

Sharing

If you choose to share a score or replay, PegJump opens your device's system share sheet. You decide which third-party app or service receives that content. Those services handle information under their own privacy policies.

Children

PegJump does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

Changes

If the app's data practices change, this policy and the app-store privacy declarations will be updated before the changed version is released.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact details on the PegJump support page.
