PegJump Support

PegJump is a one-touch pendulum game for iPhone, iPad and Android.

How to play

Hold anywhere to lasso the nearest cyan peg ahead of you. Keep holding to jet the swing forward, then release to fly. Use the Course button to enter a repeatable seed phrase or return to the daily course.

Troubleshooting

If sound does not begin, tap the screen again or toggle Sound off and on. If progress needs to be reset, delete and reinstall the app or clear its local storage in your device settings.

Contact

Report problems through the project's GitHub issue tracker.
